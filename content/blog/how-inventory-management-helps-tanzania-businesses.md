Businesses in Tanzania face unique challenges: manual stock counts, spreadsheets that don’t talk to each other, and lost sales when items are out of stock. An **inventory management system** can turn this around.

## Why inventory software matters

Manual tracking leads to:

- **Overstocking** — capital tied up in goods that sit in the warehouse  
- **Stockouts** — lost sales and unhappy customers when popular items run out  
- **Errors** — wrong counts and misplaced items that hurt both operations and reporting  

A dedicated system gives you a single source of truth: what you have, where it is, and when to reorder.

## What to look for in a system

When evaluating inventory software for your business, consider:

1. **Real-time stock levels** — updates as sales and restocks happen  
2. **Low-stock alerts** — so you reorder before you run out  
3. **Reporting** — fast-moving items, slow movers, and value of stock on hand  
4. **Integration** — with your point-of-sale or accounting tools where possible
